HOW
How We Do It

Through Science-Backed Integrated Pest Management

We follow the globally recognised ERDM Methodology — a structured, evidence-based approach that minimises chemicals while maximising results.

  • Exclusion — sealing entry points to prevent pest access
  • Restriction — eliminating food, water & harborage conditions
  • Destruction — targeted treatments by certified PMPs
  • Monitoring — ongoing inspection, documentation & follow-up
